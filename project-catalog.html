Catalog Redesign

Design Strategy Workshop Concept Testing

Overview

This project focused on redesigning the product catalog experience to improve user engagement and conversion rates. Through stakeholder interviews, user research, and iterative design, we created a more intuitive and visually appealing catalog interface.

Problem

Users were having difficulty finding products and navigating the existing catalog structure. High bounce rates indicated a poor first impression, and analytics showed low engagement with product details.

Solution

We redesigned the catalog with improved search functionality, better visual hierarchy, and clearer navigation paths. A workshop with cross-functional teams helped align stakeholders on the vision.

Results

Post-launch analytics showed significant improvements in user engagement, time on page, and conversion rates. User feedback was overwhelmingly positive.
